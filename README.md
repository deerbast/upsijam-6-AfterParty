**Burn with fire...**

Last night, your parents were away… so you threw the wildest party of your life.

Loud music, too many guests, trash everywhere, and a backyard that looks like a complete disaster. At the time, it was worth it.

Then your phone rings.

It’s your mom. She tells you that she and your dad are coming home early from their vacation… and they’re already on their way.

Panic hits instantly.

The backyard is a total mess, and there’s no way you can clean everything before they arrive. There’s only one desperate solution left:

**BURN EVERYTHING IN THE FIRE.**

You play as a teenage boy who must get rid of all the evidence before his parents make it back home. Grab the trash, throw it in, burn it fast, and do whatever it takes to hide the chaos.

But be careful: if your parents catch you before the mess is gone, you’re doomed.

No more going out. No more freedom. And all your stuff? Confiscated.

How far will you go to erase the evidence?
